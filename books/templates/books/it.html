Urgent College Safety Alert!

On university campuses across America, reports are skyrocketing of students disappearing into sewer drains, lured by a mysterious clown-like figure offering free textbooks. Don't let IT happen to you!

It was 3:37 pm last Tuesday when Julie Stephens, a sophomore attending Miami Dade College, received a troubling text from her roommate, Heather Furman. "Heather had really been worried about how she was going to pay for her textbooks this term." Julie told us. "But after my last class dismissed, she texted me saying. 'Who knew you could get free textbooks in the sewer?' I rushed home just in time to see her lowering herself into the storm drain outside our apartment building. I haven't see her since! I'm really worried!"

Julie is not alone. Ross Simpworth has a son, Rob, studying Business Management at Texas A&M University. We reached Ross by phone:

"Rob's been calling letting me know that he needs a $240 textbook. I told him that I'd send him money as soon as I got paid. But then, he left a voicemail telling us not to worry about it, he'd found someone on campus who could get him the book for free. I was worried that he was planning to download an illegal pdf off the internet, I never would have guessed he'd stuck some evil bargain with a sewer-dwelling clown! I'll never forgive myself for what has happened!"
